Volcan Cia. Minera S.A.A. (BVL: VOLCABC1)

Volcan Compania Minera SAA is a polymetallic miner having 3 mining units (Yauli, and Cerro de Pasco Chungar) located in Peru. The company has reserves to ensure a life of mine of 15 years, and in 2012 produced 22 million ounces of silver. In 2014, operations will begin its new mining unit Alpamarca - Pallanga River and silver oxide project in your unit Cerro de Pasco, which together account for 8 million additional silver per year.
