Ferreycorp S.A.A. (BVL: FERREYC1)

Ferreycorp is a conglomerate of companies whose primary line of business is the distribution of Caterpillar machinery and equipment (primarily through its subsidiary Ferreyros SA). Actively involved in the mining and construction sectors, where they are leaders for market share. Although the sale of machinery is the reporting unit's revenue, the company is focused on its line of post-sales services, which generate added value for its customers and for the holding attractive margins.
