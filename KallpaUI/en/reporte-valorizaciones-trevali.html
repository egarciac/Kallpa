Trevali Mining Corp. (TSX: TV, BVL: TV)

Trevali is a Canadian producer of base metals mining company with operations in Canada and Peru. In Peru began production of zinc concentrates and lead-silver mine in the Santander in August 2013 through a plant of 2,000 TPD. Canada has several projects, among which Halfmile, Caribou and Stratmat. The 3 would begin production in late 2014.
