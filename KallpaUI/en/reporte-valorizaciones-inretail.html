InRetail Perú Corp. (BVL: INRETC1)

InRetail is a holding company comprised of three business units that are leaders in the retail sector Peruvian supermarkets, pharmacies and shopping centers. It was recently listed on the LSE (October 2012) and is one of the few investment options with 100% exposure to the Peruvian consumption. The company is part of Intercorp Group and is on an aggressive expansion process to harness the potential of the low penetration of the formal retail market in Peru.
