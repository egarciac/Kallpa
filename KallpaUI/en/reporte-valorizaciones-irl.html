Minera IRL Limited (BVL: MIRL, AIM: MIRL, TSX: IRL)

Minera IRL is a mining company focused on the development of its various precious metals assets in Latin America. Has the operating mine in Peru Corihuami with a production target of 24,000 ounces of gold per year. The flagship project of the company is Ollachea, located in Puno, Peru, to start operations by 2015. In Patagonia, Argentina also have a Don Nicolas Project, EIA 100% approved and funded.
