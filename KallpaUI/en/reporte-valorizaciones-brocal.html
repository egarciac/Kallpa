Sociedad Minera El Brocal S.A.A. (BVL: BROCALC1)

Sociedad Minera El Brocal SAA is a Peruvian polymetallic mining operations in developing Marcapunta Norte mine (copper) and Tajo Norte (polymetallic). The company has reserves that ensure the continuity of its operations for 10 years. In 2014, the expansion project at the Tajo Norte mine, which will increase its treatment capacity of 11,000 TPD to 18,000 TPD will end.
