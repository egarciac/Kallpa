Credicorp Ltd. (NYSE: BAP, BVL: BAP)

Credicorp is the nation's largest financial holding company. You have control over the Bank of Credit, a leading bank that concentrates more than 30% of loans and deposits in the local banking system. The bank's strategy is focused on increasing its share in the retail segment (consumption, medium, small and micro enterprises) market, so as to benefit from the growth of the Peruvian economy and low banking penetration (compared to other Latin American countries) . Pacific Insurance Group (insurance), Prima AFP (pension fund), Atlantic Security Bank (private banking) and Credicorp Capital (investment banking) complement the financial group.
