Trading and Brokerage Management

Its object is the purchase / sale of financial instruments in stock exchanges and the over the counter markets of Peru and abroad. This division has specialized executives in three areas: Research, Brokerage and trading.

Research area makes recommendations for purchase / sale of securities in Peru based on fundamental analysis of shares including business valuations for dividend discount models, models valuations using market ratios, technical analysis and others. This area makes a daily investment of market situation in Peru and abroad and provides information and advice on the area of brokerage investment analysis. Research has a team leader, 2 analysts and assistant.

The area of Brokerage the team is responsible for advising clients on SAB Kallpa purchase / sale of securities in Peru and abroad. The executives of this area absorb information and recommendations given by Research and place this information and recommendations to clients. To do this, the broker must know their customers, know the degree of risk aversion of themselves, their investment horizons and their tastes and preferences in the market. The relationship we build with clients brokers Kallpa SAB is vital to ensure the business of the company in the long run.

The area of Trading receives and executes the purchase / sale of shares in Peru and abroad that made ​​customers. Traders are intended to buy / sell securities at the best market prices, while respecting the customer order. Also traders will place orders for purchase / sale of securities in the international markets and cultivate relationships with international brokers.

Areas of Trading and Brokerage working together on specific operations and Tender Offers (IPOs), Public Sales Offers (IPOs), primary share issues, share placements in the secondary market, among other operations.
