Southern Copper Corp. (NYSE: SCCO, BVL: SCCO)

Southern Copper Corporation is a mining company that has operations in Peru and Mexico. It is one of the most profitable copper companies worldwide due to its low cost and has cash reserves to ensure a mine life of 100 years. The company has adopted an interesting portfolio of projects (U.S. $ 6.800 million), which will allow you to increase copper production by 60% until 2017.
