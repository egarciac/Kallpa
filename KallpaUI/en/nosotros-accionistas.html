Shareholders and Directors

Alberto Arispe Bazán

He is CEO Kallpa Securities SAB. Alberto has an MBA from the Stern School of Business at New York University and a Bachelor in Economics from the Pontificia Universidad Catolica del Peru. Has 16 years experience in the international financial system. Between 2005-2008 he was Manager of Development Projects Lima Stock Exchange. During the years 2000-2005 was Vice President of Emerging Markets Division based in New York, U.S. investment bank Fox-Pitt, Kelton Inc., a subsidiary of Swiss Re Corp. Previously, he was an investment analyst and trader Macrovalores SAB shares, a company of the Macro group. Since 2005 he is professor of the course of Securities Market of the School of Business at the University of Lima.

Enrique Hernandez Martinez

He is manager of the division of trading and brokerage Kallpa Securities SAB. Enrique holds a degree in Economics from the Pontificia Universidad Catolica del Peru, majoring in Finance. He has 20 years of experience in the domestic financial system. In the last fifteen years he has been General Manager of South American Bag SAB, Director of the Bolsa de Valores de Lima, Chief Financial Officer of Centura SAB, Financial Advisor Private Banking Citibank Lima, Business Manager Macrovalores SAB, Chief Investments in foreign currency section of the Office of International Operations and Operator BCR Wheel bag (Equities / Fixed Income) Argos SA, House bag. Since 2006 he is professor hired Bursen, market School Lima Stock Exchange.

Ricardo Carrión Kuriyama

He is manager of the Capital Markets division Kallpa Securities SAB. Ricardo holds a degree in Business Administration from the University of Lima. He has 8 years of experience in commercial banking, corporate finance and capital markets. Before creating Kallpa SAB worked as a corporate banking analyst and then as a Senior Analyst Corporate Finance and Capital Markets at Banco de Credito del Peru for almost 8 years. Has direct experience in the structuring and placement of all types of debt and equity for different industries (corporate bonds, asset-backed securities, structured bonds, mortgage bonds, leasing bonds, commercial paper, acquisitions, IPO's, etc.) and has been involved in all equity and debt transactions settled in Peru. In 2007 he was appointed as an external consultant for the Lima Stock Exchange.

Pablo Berckholtz Velarde

Is Chairman of the Board of Kallpa Securities SAB, attorney Paul is a graduate of the University of Notre Dame, Illinois and Bachelor of Theology at Georgetown University, Washington DC Since 2004 he is principal partner at Echecopar and has led the legal teams for much of the financial transactions made in Peru. Currently a member of the Board of Fisheries Exalmar SA and Board member of Procapitales. He has 15 years of experience in the international capital market. Between 2003 and 2008 he was an associate of the firm of Clifford Chance U.S., based in New York, United States. Paul is a member of the Lima Bar Association and the Bar Association of New York. He has lectured extensively in Peru and abroad.

Elizabeth Cueva Gárate

He is Managing Director of Management and Administration Support Kallpa Securities SAB. Elizabeth has a Master's Degree in Strategic Business Administration awarded by the Pontifical Catholic University of Peru and is Certified Public Accountant from the University San Martin de Porres. He has over 18 years experience in the domestic financial system. Between 2005 and 2009 he was Head of Securities SA Compania Minera Atacocha In 2000 and 2005 he was Managing Director of Operations and Compliance Officer for the Prevention of Money Laundering Bag Sudamericano SA Between 1994 and 2000 he was Chief Accountant and Operations Macrovalores SAB SA, a company in the Macro group. He also served as consultant and external Professor of Market Studies Center of the Lima Stock Exchange (Bursen).
